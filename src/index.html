Why Modbus TCP?

Modbus TCP is an open, globally accepted, simple, vendor-neutral communication protocol. Being on the top 4 of industrial ethernet protocols, Modbus TCP is a ‘de-facto’ automation standard. If your system is not deterministic and doesn’t require high-level real-time performance, it’s not reasonable to employ high-end ethernet protocols, such as EtherCat, Profinet, Ethernet/IP.
